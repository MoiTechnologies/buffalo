Buffalo Privacy Policy 1. Introduction Buffalo ("Ride with ease") operates a ride-hailing platform in Uganda. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when using our app, website, or services (collectively, "Services"). By using Buffalo, you consent to these practices. If you disagree, please discontinue use. 2. Information We Collect A. Information You Provide Account Information: Name, phone number, profile photo Identity Verification: Government ID (for drivers/rider verification) Trip Details: Pickup/drop-off locations, special requests Communications: Support messages, feedback B. Automatic Collection Location Data Foreground: Precise GPS when app is active (for ride matching) Background: For drivers during active trips only, to: ✓ Maintain real-time trip tracking ✓ Enable navigation while using other apps ✓ Ensure passenger safety and accurate fare calculation You can disable background location in device settings, but this will severely limit app functionality. Usage Data: Trip history, app interactions C. Third-Party Information Referral programs Payment processors (Mobile Money/banks) Legal authorities (when required) 3. How We Use Your Information Purpose Data Used Provide Services Account info, location, trip details Safety & Fraud Prevention Identity verification, device data Customer Support Contact details, trip records Service Improvements Usage analytics Legal Compliance As required by Ugandan law 4. Data Sharing We only share necessary data with: Drivers: Your name, pickup location (never full address) Payment processors: Transaction confirmations Authorities: When legally mandated We never sell your data. 5. Security Measures End-to-end encryption for all data transfers Regular security audits Strict access controls 6. Your Rights ✔ Access/correct your data via account settings ✔ Delete account (residual data retained for legal compliance) ✔ Opt-out of marketing communications ✔ Disable location tracking (limits functionality) 7. Data Retention Active accounts: Until deletion request Trip records: 12 months (dispute resolution) Legal requirements: As per Ugandan law 8. Background Location Justification For drivers, background location access is essential to: Maintain trip continuity if you switch to other apps (calls/maps) Ensure passenger safety through real-time tracking Calculate accurate fares based on actual route taken Enable emergency assistance if needed This aligns with Google Play's policy for critical functionality. 9.
